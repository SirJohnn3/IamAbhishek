Master's in Bioengineering, focused on research with expertise in probe design and development

Hi, I'm Abhishek Kumar, an M.Tech student in Bioengineering at IIT Bhilai focusing on developing a probe for early detection of breast cancer. My research involves biomarker design, solid-phase peptide synthesis, and nanoparticle analysis.

I've gained diverse research experience through my work at Queen's University Belfast studying EPCR in diabetes, and at AIIMS Raipur where I trained in biomedical instrumentation and radiology. My B.Tech research at Shoolini University involved analyzing biofilms of S. typhi.

I'm particularly skilled in biomedical imaging techniques like MRI, with expertise in contrast agent development, relaxometry measurements, and phantom imaging. I combine this with advanced knowledge of FTIR, NMR, DLS, and microscopy to bridge biotechnology and medical science for innovative healthcare solutions.

Beyond research, I have a strong entrepreneurial mindset, having been recognized in innovation competitions like HackTheHurdle and placing in the top 15 teams at Ideathon. I'm driven to transform scientific discoveries into real-world healthcare solutions that can make a meaningful impact.
